## Research Experience

### Graduate Research Assistant, Wireless@VT
*Virginia Tech, Blacksburg/Alexandria, VA — Jan. 2022 -- Present*

**O-RAN-based 4G/5G testbeds**

* Implemented 4G and 5G USRP-based hardware testbeds with srsRAN and OpenAirInterface (OAI) protocol stacks.
* Deployed deep reinforcement learning-based network slicing and handover-management xApps through RIC on 4G and 5G testbeds.

**AI for non-terrestrial networks**

* Conducted a comprehensive literature survey on potential AI applications for satellite-based NTN challenges in 6G networks.
* Implemented network slicing from delayed network state information in an O-RAN-enabled NTN architecture using AI models.
* Implemented a decentralized deep reinforcement learning-based handover optimization framework considering link, trajectory, and load conditions, evaluated in ns-3 with realistic adaptations.

**Spatial spectrum mapping and access**

* Implemented a graph-based recurrent learning approach that replaces costly backpropagation with one-shot readout training for low-complexity, real-time spatiotemporal prediction validated on ns-3.
* Performed theoretical analysis showing that node states are influenced by spatially weighted and temporally delayed node interactions, aligning with wireless propagation characteristics.
