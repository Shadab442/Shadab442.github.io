**Graduate Researcher, Virginia Tech Wireless@VT**<br>
AI/ML for wireless networking · O-RAN · 5G/6G · Non-terrestrial networks · Spectrum sharing

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Computer Engineering at **Virginia Tech**, working with **Wireless@VT** on AI-enabled resource management for next-generation wireless systems. My research focuses on applying machine learning, reinforcement learning, and network optimization to **O-RAN**, **terrestrial and non-terrestrial networks**, **spectrum sharing**, **network slicing**, and **handover management**.

I build and evaluate wireless systems across both simulation and testbed environments, including **ns-3**, **USRP-based 4G/5G testbeds**, **srsRAN**, **OpenAirInterface**, and **O-RAN RIC/xApps**.

## Research Areas

| Area | Current Focus |
| --- | --- |
| **AI for Wireless Networks** | Reinforcement learning and deep learning for resource management, scheduling, and handover decisions. |
| **O-RAN and 5G/6G Testbeds** | xApp-based network slicing and handover control on USRP-based 4G/5G platforms. |
| **Non-Terrestrial Networks** | AI-enabled NTN architectures, delayed state information, satellite mobility, and terrestrial-NTN integration. |
| **Spectrum Mapping and Access** | Graph-based recurrent learning for real-time spatiotemporal spectrum prediction. |
| **Edge Caching** | Decentralized and online video caching algorithms for small-cell edge networks. |
