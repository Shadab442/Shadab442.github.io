## Privacy

This website is a personal academic site. It is intended to share research, publications, talks, teaching experience, and contact information.

At the repository level, the site does not maintain a comment system, account system, or custom user database. If platform providers such as GitHub Pages or your browser collect operational data, that behavior is governed by their own policies rather than by custom tracking code in this repository.

If you contact me by email, your message is used only for direct correspondence.
